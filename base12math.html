Understanding Base-12 Math in Universum

Forming Numbers

In Universum, numbers are formed using a base-12 system. Here are some examples:

  • pali (1 in decimal)
  • telo (2 in decimal)
  • telo lana pali (25 in decimal, which is two twelves plus one)

Basic Arithmetic Operations

Arithmetic operations in Universum follow the base-12 system. Examples include:

  • telo + telo = luko (2 + 2 = 4 in decimal)
  • kuna * telo = telo lana (4 * 2 = 24 in decimal)
  • telo lana - telo = pali lana pali (24 - 2 = 22 in decimal)

Higher Numbers and Place Value

For numbers larger than 12, place value increases by multiples of 12 (lana). Examples:

  • pali lana lana (144 in decimal, or 12^2)
  • telo lana lana pali lana (289 in decimal, or 2x12^2 + 12)
