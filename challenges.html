Universum Challenges

Here, you can contribute to Universum as a whole.

Translation Trials

Translate texts from various languages into Universum. The best translations will be added to the official corpus.

Lexicon Expansion Quests

Propose new words or phrases for concepts lacking in Universum. Successful entries will be officially added to the language.

Dialogue Duels

Write and submit dialogues using Universum around provided themes. Winning entries will be used as model dialogues for teaching.

Cultural Integration Challenges

Create content that integrates Universum into specific cultural contexts. Winning entries will be featured prominently.

Grammar Games

Develop puzzles or games that help users practice and master complex grammatical structures of Universum.
